Recipies

  • Chinese Chicken and Ramen
  • Funfetti Cupcakes
  • Watermelon Slushi

How to make it:

  • Chinese Chicken and Ramen: Marinate chicken with soy sauce, ginger, garlic, brown sugar, and spice. Cook to desired temperature. Boil ramen and add seasonings. Enjoy! :)


  • Funfetti Cupcakes: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, butter, vanilla, milk, eggs, and sprinkles. Bake for twenty minutes, cool, then pipe frosting on top.


  • Watermelon Slushi: Cut up watermelon and lightly blend. Add sprite/ 7up. The ratio should be 3:1
